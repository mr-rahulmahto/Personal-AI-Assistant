Here's a sample letter for a School Leaving Certificate Application:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

The Principal,
[Name of School]
[School Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Leaving Certificate

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to inform you that I, Rahul Mahto, a student of Standard [X/XII] in your esteemed institution, wish to request a leaving certificate. As per the school's rules and regulations, I am required to submit a formal application for the same.

I have been a part of this school community since [Year] and have had a wonderful experience during my time here. I am grateful for the knowledge, skills, and values that I have acquired during my tenure at [Name of School]. However, due to [Reasons for leaving, e.g. family transfer, personal reasons, etc.], I am required to take my studies elsewhere.

I would like to request a leaving certificate as per the norms of the school, which would be useful for me to pursue further studies or other academic endeavors. I have fulfilled all my academic obligations and have cleared all my dues with the school.

I would like to request you to issue the leaving certificate at the earliest, as I need it for my future academic pursuits. I shall be grateful if you could provide me with a leaving certificate within [Timeframe, e.g. 7-10 days] from the date of this application.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to receiving my leaving certificate soon.

Yours faithfully,

Rahul Mahto
[Your Class and Roll Number]
[Your Date of Birth]

Please note that you should modify the letter according to your specific requirements and school policies.
